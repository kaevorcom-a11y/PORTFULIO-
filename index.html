Frequently Asked Questions সাধারণ প্রশ্নোত্তর

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

T-Shirt: 500 pcs | Shirt: 300 pcs | Panjabi: 200 pcs

What is the estimated production time?

Our standard production time is 30-45 days after sample approval and deposit.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples. The sample cost is refundable upon placing a bulk order.

What are your payment methods?

We accept L/C (Letter of Credit) and TT (Telegraphic Transfer) with 30% advance payment.
